About us

FishExperts is a dedicated team of skilled guides, fishermen, sailors
taking you to the best fishing spots in San Diego.

You can catch fish with your own hands under the guidance of our experienced
fishermen. The guides will tell you a lot of interesting stories about the
surroundings of San Diego and types of the local fish. You can take the catch
home, or our chefs will cook the caught fish on the grill.



Our History

Fishing in San Diego may not be obvious, but it's worth coming to California.
We've had a long way to start our company and offer our customers a wide range of services.

September 2000

Our owner, Steve Marston, became
interested in fishing when he bought a
ranch in California close to the water.

May 2002

Steve caught the biggest red snapper
in his career so far - 13 pounds! This
happened by pure chance near
Steve's house.

May 2005

Steve started individual guided tours
in San Diego, as he wanted more
people to try fishing in these great
spots.

June 2017

FishExperts was launched. Now
we have more than 12 types of guided
tours in the San Diego surroundings.

How to Go on Fishing?

We offer tours all year round. Our motor boats can accommodate up to 10 people,
so don't forget to book your tour in advance.

Step 1. Contact us

Call us, write us or simply fill out the contact form, and we'll get in touch as soon
as possible to select the best tour for you.

Step 2. Book your spot

As a guarantee of the reservation, we ask our customers to make an advance
payment of 50% of the total amount.

Step 3. Meet us at the pier

Our guide will meet you at pier 3 in San Diego and take you on a boat, give the
equipment and accompany you on an exciting trip!

Safety and
Responsibility

All of our tours are 100% safe both for adults and kids.

All our motor boats pass all the necessary checks. We guarantee your
safety on board. We have a license for tourism services and fishing in
the places where we take our customers. Even if you have never held
a fishing rod in your hands, we will teach you everything and do
everything possible so that you get the most out of your trip.

We adhere to the schedule as far as practicable, keep an eye on your
children and amuse them during the tour.
